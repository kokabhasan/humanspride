About Human's Pride

Human’s Pride is an organization that aims to deal with the upliftment of the downtrodden section of our society regarding education, health and awareness.

Human’s Pride has grown from an idea into an organization. A group of students shared a dream and to fulfill it they took the initiative to ignite action. Based on human values, the objectives are to foster change will benefit not only individuals, but humanity as a whole.

We believe that by taking in the young blood in combination with the experienced, we will have a fusion of thoughts and idea. The members will work together to the poor and afflicted in all aspects and thereby creating a better world.

Our Mission is

  • to work towards child labour eradication,
  • to provide free education and schooling to the ‘Little Indians’,
  • to sensitize general masses about vacuum between needs and its fulfillment
  • to create awareness about socio-economic conditions in the slum areas to bring forward Sir Syed legacy into 21st century, the dream of his literacy mission, the zeal of his workaholic nature.

We have registered membership of more than 300 individuals. They have zeal and agree to be with us in our endeavors. We invite people to join us as members to sustain and spread our ideas and work. We are also indebted to our reverent teachers for supporting our cause.
